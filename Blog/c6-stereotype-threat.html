Stereotype Threat

4-22-15

Stereotype threat occurs when someone is feeling at risk of being judged based on a sterotype, and possibly proving the sterotype true, and because of this fear they perform worse than they would if this fear wasn't in their head. I don't feel very comfortable going into much detail on my assumptions on how a sterotype feels and what may come from stereotype threat. I think we can make the DBC workplace stereotype free, at least I know I will try to do my part in opening up to everyone and treating everyone with equal respect. Any assumpions I have on indiviuals prior to attending DBC will be based on small samples from Phase 0, and even those I will try to forget about as we head to Chicago. My apologies for the short post, but I just don't feel comfortable going any deeper into this topic. I will, however, read the thoughts of others to help me see things from their perspective. I feel that's important on topics like this.
